[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Instrumental intro – warm acoustic strumming]
I trace the shadows dancing on your skin
A slow fire burning where the dark begins
I pull you closer, let the whole world fade
Lost in the beautiful mess we made

[Verse 1]
The moon is spilling through the window pane
Washing the covers like a silver rain
You whisper softly, pulling at my shirt
Taking away every kind of hurt

[Pre-Chorus]
There is a fire in the way you breathe
Tying your quietest desires to me
Letting the silence do the talking now
I want to memorize you, show you how

[Chorus]
[Building rhythm]
Oh, these endless nights with you
Tangled in the dark, nothing else to do
Just my hands on your waist and your lips on mine
Freezing the clock, running out of time

(Endless nights, oh)
[Whispered] (Yeah, freezing the clock right here)

[Chorus]

[Whispered] (Yeah, freezing the clock right here)

[Bridge]
[Guitar solo – tender and slow, two bars]
Oh, these endless nights with you
Nothing else that I would ever want to do
When the morning tries to steal the dark away
I will pull you closer, make the moment stay
You know Saeed will always keep you near
Holding every whisper safe and spoken here

[Spoken]
I never want this night to end
Just stay right here with me
